About

Hi — I'm Atharv Gupta. a B.Com (Hons) student at Delhi University, fitness enthusiast, and content creator. I balance academics in subjects like Indian Economy and GST & Customs Law with my passion for fitness and nutrition, where I share a 100-day fitness series on Instagram to simplify workouts, diet, and lifestyle for beginners. What defines me is discipline and adaptability—from cooking high-protein meals on a student budget without a fridge, to creating aesthetic fitness photos with low-cost setups. I believe in turning limitations into strengths, staying consistent, and inspiring others through my journey. My vision is simple: grow, inspire, and build opportunities where commerce, creativity, and fitness meet.
